RestoFlow is not just a POS; it is an **AI-Driven Restaurant Operating System**. It moves the industry from *Recording* data to *Interpreting* and *Acting* upon it autonomously.

---

### 🧠 1. THE BRAIN: AI CHAT AGENT
**Manage your restaurant through conversation, not spreadsheets.**
*   **The Difference:** Most systems require you to pull 10+ reports to find an answer. With RestoFlow, you simply ask the AI: *"What is my most profitable dish this month?"* or *"Who is my fastest-serving waiter?"*.

### 🔮 2. AI SALES & LABOUR FORECASTING
**Predicting profit, preventatively.**
*   **The Difference:** Instead of overstaffing "just in case," our AI uses years of historical patterns and external data (weather, events) to predict sales with **94%+ accuracy**.

*   **The Result:** Continuous margin protection without requiring manual oversight.

## 🏗️ THE "TECHNICAL MAGIC": HOW IT WORKS

RestoFlow isn't just a wrapper; it's a deep-integration engine. Here is how the data flows and how the AI actually "thinks."

### 🔄 1. The Unified Data Lake
Every module (POS, Inventory, Staff) writes to a single, interconnected database.
*   **The Flow:** When a waiter sells a "Beef Burger," the system instantly:
    1.  Deducts 150g of Beef and 1 Brioche Bun from **Inventory** (Live Depletion).
    2.  Calculates the **Profit Margin** of that specific plate in real-time.
    3.  Adds the sales data to the **Server Performance** dashboard.

### 🧠 2. AI Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
How does the AI Answer your questions?
*   **The Logic:** We don't just "talk" to a chatbot; we use **RAG**. When you ask a question, the AI pulls the relevant data tables (e.g., `orders`, `ingredients`) into its context window, analyzes the numbers, and generates an answer that is 100% grounded in your actual business numbers. No hallucinations—just hard data.

### 🌓 3. Proactive Monitoring (The Watchdog)
How does the "Nudge" work?
*   **The Logic:** A constant background worker (Watchdog) runs comparison loops:
    *   **Loop:** *Sales Trend* vs. *Shift Cost*.
    *   **Loop:** *Market Price API* vs. *Vendor Price List*.
*   If the difference (variance) crosses a set threshold, the system triggers an event that "injects" a prompt into the Manager's dashboard instantly.
